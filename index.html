About Me

Hi there, I'm Aladin Mutuma from Kenya! I'm a web developer, and I'm passionate about creating engaging and user-friendly websites. My skill set includes proficiency in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Ruby, SQL, and Kubernetes. I enjoy exploring new technologies and finding creative solutions to challenging problems. With my expertise in web development, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality and efficient solutions to help businesses achieve their goals.

As a web developer, I understand front-end development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm well-versed in implementing responsive web design techniques, optimizing website performance, and ensuring cross-browser compatibility.

In addition to my front-end skills, I have expertise in server-side technologies such as Ruby and SQL. I am proficient in building and maintaining robust back-end infrastructures that support the functionality of web applications. I'm also familiar with Kubernetes, a popular container orchestration tool that allows for efficient deployment and management of web applications.

My proficiency in these technologies enables me to build dynamic and scalable web applications that meet the needs of businesses across various industries. I'm always eager to learn and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in the web development industry. With my skills and experience, I am confident in my ability to deliver high-quality solutions to clients and contribute to the growth of the web development community.
